I am a Graduate Student in the College of Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology pursuing a Masters in Computer Science.

I am also a recent graduate from Western Governors Univeristy with a Bachelors in Computer Science.

I am specializing in Machine Learning with my research and a subsequent focus on Computing Systems including Operating Systems, Compilers, and Embedded Systems.

My coursework focuses on deep learning, computer vision, high-performance computer architecture, computer networks, embedded systems, compilers, operating systems, reinforced learning.

In my free time I have a passion for hacking, breaking software, and reverse engineering video games.

Originally, I am from South Carolina but for the duration of my undergrad I lived in Arizona. I am now back on the east coast while I finish my graduate degree!

When I am not at my home lab building I am outside running, hiking and working out. I love traveling and trying new experiences out.
